Overview

The above workbook looks at the advertising trends of drug manufacturers to NPIs in Bloomington, Indiana. I utilize a data set called Open Payments, which is a public use CMS dataset that documents all advertising payment encounters between an NPI and a drug manufacturer in the US. each Data set is published yearly, and is approximately 6 million row of payment encounters. For this analysis I utilized SQL to clean and filter the open payments Data to only show locations in Bloomington, IN. I then completed the descriptive analysis on SQL, producing overall advertising trends, the most popular form of advertising in each year quarter, and the most frequent medications advertised. Through this analysis I find that Skyrizi and Vraylar are the most advertised medicine in Bloomington overall. To further explore these medications I look at their quarterly advertising trends and find that, locally, both medications spike in advertising late 2021 and early 2022. I then compared these to direct to consumer advertising trends, which I get from a source called media radar. In the direct to consumer advertising trends I see that advertising spikes in late 2023 for both medications. This suggests that local doctors recieve drug advertising sooner then advertising done directly to the consumer.
